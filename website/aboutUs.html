Our Mission

SMUve is intended to make the lives of college students less stressful by simplifying the moving and furnishing process.SMUve turns your college campus into a marketplace! You can buy and sell anything from textbooks to kitchen sets. Are you away for summer break but you see a deal you can not pass up? SMUve will connect you with a driver in the area to pick it up and store it until you get back! Need to make an extra buck? You can offer the same service to other students! Lets get your next semester SMUving!
